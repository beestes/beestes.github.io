About

I'm Blake Estes, a passionate graphic designer from Saint Louis. I currently have an AAS in Graphic Communications and am pursing a BFA in Graphic Design at University of Missouri - Saint Louis. While continuing my studies, I am actively building my knowledge in various graphic and web design techniques through programs such as The Odin Project, Skillshare, and Codecademy. For hobbies, I find enjoyment in photography and music.

Project created in 2018, following the theme of my personal logo; structure with a little bit of chaos. Hand crafted and photographed from glass materials. Based on Baskerville Bold. The project has been used to promote recycling, as the name suggests, and to bring attention to sustainability.

Data Visualization

Data visualization created in 2018 for a project showcasing differences and similaraties in Haiti and Hawaii. Typography choice of Futura and illustrations created in Illustrator via the use of the pen and blend tools; all images created as vector objects.

Logo

Personal logo created in 2018; structure with a little bit of chaos. I’ve found in my life that keeping my surroundings extremely organized, clean, and minimal help me remain focused. Internally, I have a habit of overthinking and overanalyzing, so this sort of structured outside is how I contain it. Additionally, the form is created out of the letter ‘E’ in lieu of my last name.

Ribbon Lettering

Ribbon lettering created in 2018 for a project title. Based off of the typeface DIN, sketched on an iPad, formed through the pen tool, and finalized in Photoshop. Calendar layout created in inDesign featuring artwork and copy covering lettering connoisseurs Jessica Hische and Luke Lucas.
